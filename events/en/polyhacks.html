PolyHacks

hosted by PolyHx


Location: Polytechnique (Montreal)

Date: Saturday, February 3rd - Sunday, February 4th

Language: English & French

Type: Hackathon

Registration deadline: January 21st

Lien: https://polyhacks.io

For the sixth year in a row, we are organizing a new edition of PolyHacks - Hackatown. It is a 48-hour programming and innovation competition where participants come to create their city in a smarter way. This colossal event is organized with Major League Hacking and the student committee of computer engineering and software of Polytechnique School of Engineering. We offer this opportunity for everyone to come up with ideas that are both ambitious and innovative.
